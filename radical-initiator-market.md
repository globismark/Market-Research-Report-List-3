<p><strong>The Radical Initiator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Halogen Molecules</li><li>Azo Compounds</li><li>Peroxides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radical initiator markets can be classified into three main categories: Halogen Molecules, Azo Compounds, and Peroxides. Halogen Molecules serve as effective radical initiators due to their ability to easily break the carbon-halogen bond and generate radicals. Azo Compounds are commonly used as radical initiators in polymerization reactions due to their stability and efficiency in generating radicals. Peroxides are highly reactive compounds that can generate radicals through homolytic cleavage, making them valuable in various industrial processes requiring radical initiation.</p></p>
