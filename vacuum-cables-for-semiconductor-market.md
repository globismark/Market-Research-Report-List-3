<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Vacuum Cables for Semiconductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Vacuum (HV)</li><li>Ultra-high Vacuum (UHV)</li><li>Extreme High Vacuum (XHV)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Vacuum (HV) cables are designed to withstand pressures below atmospheric pressure but above 10^-3 Torr. Ultra-high Vacuum (UHV) cables are used in environments with pressures below 10^-6 Torr, providing enhanced performance and reliability. Extreme High Vacuum (XHV) cables are engineered for extremely low pressures below 10^-9 Torr, ensuring the highest level of cleanliness and outgassing for critical semiconductor applications. Each type of vacuum cable is tailored to specific pressure ranges to meet the unique requirements of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
