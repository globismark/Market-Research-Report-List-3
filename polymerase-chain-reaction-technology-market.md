<p><p>Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) technology market includes the Digital PCR and Real-Time PCR segments. Digital PCR allows for precise quantification of nucleic acids by dividing a sample into thousands of individual reactions. Real-Time PCR, on the other hand, provides continuous monitoring of the amplification process, enabling real-time measurement of DNA amplification. Both technologies are widely used in research, diagnostics, and forensics due to their high sensitivity, accuracy, and speed in amplifying and detecting DNA and RNA sequences.</p></p>
