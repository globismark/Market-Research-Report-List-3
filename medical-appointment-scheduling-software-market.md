<p><p>Medical appointment scheduling software is available in two main market types: web-based and on-premise. Web-based software is accessed through a web browser, allowing users to schedule appointments from any device with internet access. This type of software is preferred for its flexibility and accessibility. On the other hand, on-premise software is installed locally on a healthcare facility's servers and accessed only on-site. This type of software offers greater control over data security and privacy but may require more maintenance and IT support.</p></p>
