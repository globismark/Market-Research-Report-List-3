<p><p>DIP & SIP switches are commonly used in electronic devices for easy configuration of various settings. The market for these switches can be segregated into SMT Type and Through Hole Type. SMT Type switches are surface-mount components that are soldered directly onto PCBs, offering space-saving and automated assembly benefits. On the other hand, Through Hole Type switches have leads that pass through holes in the PCB for a strong mechanical connection. Both types cater to different application needs and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
