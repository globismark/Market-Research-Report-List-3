There are two main types of veterinary infusion pumps: large-volume infusion pumps and syringe infusion pumps. Large-volume infusion pumps are used for continuous infusion of fluids and medications in large volumes, while syringe infusion pumps are used for more precise delivery of small volumes of fluids and medications. Both types of infusion pumps play a crucial role in maintaining hydration, delivering medication, and providing critical care to animals in veterinary settings.</p></p>
