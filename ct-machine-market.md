<p><p>CT Machine Market Types refer to different technologies used in computed tomography machines. Non-Spiral Scan CT machines use a traditional scanning method, capturing images one slice at a time. Single-Slice Spiral Scan CT machines can capture multiple slices simultaneously, providing faster and more detailed images. Multi-Slice Spiral Scan CT machines are the most advanced, allowing for the capture of multiple slices in one rotation, resulting in even faster imaging and improved diagnostic accuracy. These market types cater to varying needs and preferences in the medical imaging industry.</p></p>
