<p><p>Railway Signaling has different market types based on the technology used. Communication-Based Train Control (CBTC) is a signaling system that uses communication between trains and control centers to manage traffic and ensure safety. Positive Train Control (PTC) is a system that automatically stops a train before accidents occur. Automatic Train Control (ATC) is a system that controls train movements without human intervention. These technologies are essential for modern railway operations to improve efficiency, safety, and reliability.</p></p>
