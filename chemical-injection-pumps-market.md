<p><p>Chemical injection pumps are commonly categorized into three main types: electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic. Electric chemical injection pumps operate using electricity, making them easy to control and operate. Pneumatic chemical injection pumps use compressed air to function, providing flexibility in remote or hazardous environments. Hydraulic chemical injection pumps utilize hydraulic fluid to power the pump, offering high reliability and precision in injecting chemicals. Each type of pump has its own unique advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements and applications.</p></p>
