<p><p>Elastic bonding sealants come in various types such as rubber type, resin type, and oil-based type. Rubber type sealants offer flexibility and adhesive properties, making them suitable for dynamic joints. Resin type sealants provide strong bonding capabilities and resistance to weathering. Oil-based type sealants offer excellent adhesion to various substrates and are often used for sealing joints in vehicles and machinery. Each type of elastic bonding sealant caters to specific application requirements, providing different benefits in terms of flexibility, strength, and durability.</p></p>
