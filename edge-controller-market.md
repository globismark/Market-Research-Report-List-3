<p><ul><li>DIN-rail</li><li>Panel Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DIN-rail and Panel Mount are two common types of Edge Controllers used in industrial automation and control systems. DIN-rail controllers are designed to be mounted on standard DIN rails within control cabinets, offering easy installation and space-saving benefits. Panel Mount controllers, on the other hand, are designed to be directly mounted on control panels or enclosures, providing a convenient solution for applications where space is limited. Both types cater to specific market needs, with DIN-rail controllers being more suitable for large-scale applications and Panel Mount controllers for smaller installations.</p></p>
