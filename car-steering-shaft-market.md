<p><p>The car steering shaft market includes various types such as rack and pinion, worm crank pin, and recirculating ball. Rack and pinion type systems use a gear mechanism to convert steering wheel movement into lateral movement. Worm crank pin type systems utilize a threaded shaft and gear to control steering. Recirculating ball type systems employ bearings to reduce friction and allow for smoother steering. Each type offers unique features and benefits to cater to different vehicle requirements.</p></p>
