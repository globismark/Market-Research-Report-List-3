<p><p>Parcel sortation systems can be categorized into two main types: Horizontal Cross Strip Sorters and Vertical Cross Strip Sorters. Horizontal Cross Strip Sorters use a series of horizontal conveyor belts to move parcels to designated chutes for sorting. On the other hand, Vertical Cross Strip Sorters utilize vertical conveyor belts to transport parcels to different levels for sorting. Both systems are commonly used in logistics centers and distribution warehouses to efficiently sort and route parcels for delivery.</p></p>
