Plastic injection involves injecting molten plastic into a mold to create a desired shape. Plastic extrusion involves pushing molten plastic through a die to create a continuous profile. Plastic machining involves cutting and shaping plastic materials using CNC machines. Blow molding involves inflating a heated plastic tube to create hollow objects. Thermoforming involves heating and shaping plastic sheets into desired forms.</p></p>
