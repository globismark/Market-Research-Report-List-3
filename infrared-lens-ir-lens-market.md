<p><p>Infrared lenses are classified into two main types: prime and zoom. Prime infrared lenses have a fixed focal length, providing a single magnification level. On the other hand, zoom infrared lenses offer variable focal lengths, allowing users to adjust the magnification level as needed. Both types of lenses are used in various applications such as surveillance, military, medical imaging, and industrial inspection. The choice between prime and zoom lenses depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
