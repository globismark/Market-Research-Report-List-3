<p><p>Fluorocarbon surfactants market can be classified into four main types based on their chemical structure and properties. Amphoteric surfactants have both positive and negative charges, making them versatile and suitable for a wide range of applications. Anionic surfactants have a negative charge and are commonly used in detergents and cleaning products. Cationic surfactants have a positive charge and are effective in conditioning agents and fabric softeners. Nonionic surfactants have no charge and are known for their excellent stability and compatibility with other chemicals.</p></p>
