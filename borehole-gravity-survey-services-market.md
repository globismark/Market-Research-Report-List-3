<p><p>Borehole Gravity Survey Services can be categorized into two main types: Absolute Gravity Gradiometer and Relative Gravity Gradiometer. The Absolute Gravity Gradiometer market involves measuring the precise acceleration due to gravity at a specific location, providing accurate gravity measurements for scientific research and geophysical exploration. On the other hand, the Relative Gravity Gradiometer market focuses on measuring the difference in gravity between two or more locations, which is useful for identifying subsurface features and anomalies in various industries such as oil and gas exploration and mineral exploration.</p></p>
