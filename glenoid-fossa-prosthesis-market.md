<p><p>Glenoid Fossa Prosthesis market offers two types of prostheses - Cemented and Cementless. Cemented prostheses are fixed in place using bone cement, providing immediate stability but requiring potential revision surgery in the future. On the other hand, Cementless prostheses rely on bone ingrowth for stability, allowing for better long-term fixation but may require longer healing time. Both types have their advantages and considerations, making them suitable for different patient needs and preferences in the Glenoid Fossa Prosthesis market.</p></p>
