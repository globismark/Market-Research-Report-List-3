Linear actuators are designed to produce linear motion, while rotary actuators produce rotary motion. Linear actuators are commonly used in applications where straight line motion is required, such as in control valves. Rotary actuators are utilized in applications where rotational motion is needed, such as in ball or butterfly valves. Both types of actuators play a crucial role in various industries such as oil & gas, water treatment, and power generation.</p></p>
