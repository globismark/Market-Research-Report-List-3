<p><p>The Anti Submarine Rocket Launching System market includes three main types of launch systems: Hot Launch, Cold Launch, and Concentric Canister Launch. In Hot Launch systems, the missile is ignited inside the launch tube before being ejected. Cold Launch systems involve ejecting the missile from the tube before ignition. Concentric Canister Launch systems use a concentric tube design for smoother ejection and reduced risk of damage to the missile. These different types cater to various requirements and preferences in the market for anti-submarine rocket launching systems.</p></p>
