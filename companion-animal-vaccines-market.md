<p><p>Companion Animal Vaccines market encompasses various types of vaccines such as Attenuated Live Vaccines, Conjugate Vaccines, Inactivated Vaccines, Subunit Vaccines, Toxoid Vaccines, DNA Vaccines, and Recombinant Vaccines. Attenuated live vaccines contain weakened forms of pathogens, while conjugate vaccines combine antigens from different sources. Inactivated vaccines are made from killed pathogens, subunit vaccines use specific parts of pathogens for immunity, toxoid vaccines target toxins produced by pathogens, DNA vaccines use genetic material for immunity, and recombinant vaccines use modified pathogens for protection.</p></p>
