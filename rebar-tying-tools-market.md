<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rebar tying tools come in two main types: Fully Automatic and Semi-automatic. Fully Automatic rebar tying tools are designed to automatically feed, twist, and cut the tie wire with minimal effort from the user, making the process faster and more efficient. On the other hand, Semi-automatic tools require the user to manually feed the wire and operate the tool to twist and cut the tie. While Semi-automatic tools may require more user involvement, they are generally more affordable and easier to use for smaller projects.</p></p>
