<p><ul><li>MEMS Based Scanning</li><li>Phase Array</li><li>Non-Scanning Flash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS based scanning in 3D solid-state LiDAR technology involves the use of miniature mechanical mirrors to steer laser beams and acquire detailed depth information. Phase array technology utilizes multiple laser transmitters and receiver modules to generate a 3D point cloud of the surrounding environment. Non-scanning flash LiDAR systems use a flash of light to capture a single frame of the entire scene simultaneously, providing real-time data acquisition. Each market type offers unique advantages and applications depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
