<p><p>Nausea medicine market can be categorized into various types based on their mode of action. Antacids work by neutralizing stomach acid to alleviate nausea. Antagonists, on the other hand, block the action of histamine to reduce acid production in the stomach. Steroids are used in more severe cases of nausea to reduce inflammation and suppress the immune response. Other types of medicines may include antiemetics, which help to prevent vomiting, and prokinetics, which promote movement in the digestive tract to alleviate nausea.</p></p>
