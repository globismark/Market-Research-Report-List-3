<p><p>Battery separator paper market can be divided into two main types: dry separator paper and wet separator paper. Dry separator paper is typically made from cellulose fibers and is used in lithium-ion batteries to physically separate the anode and cathode while allowing the flow of ions. On the other hand, wet separator paper is impregnated with electrolyte solution and is commonly used in lead-acid batteries to prevent short circuits and facilitate the movement of ions. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and safety of batteries.</p></p>
