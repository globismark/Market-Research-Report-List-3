<p><p>Ceiling-mounted Surgical Lights are essential equipment in operating rooms, providing bright and adjustable lighting for surgical procedures. Two main types of configurations exist - One Arm and Two Arm. One Arm lights have a single light source attached to a single arm, offering flexibility and easy maneuverability in smaller spaces. Two Arm lights feature dual light sources mounted on two separate arms, providing a larger coverage area and more precise positioning for complex surgeries. Both types of lights play a crucial role in ensuring optimal visibility during surgical procedures.</p></p>
