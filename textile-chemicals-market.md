<p><p>Textile chemicals market is segmented into categories such as Chemical Fiber Oil, Printing Auxiliaries, Pretreatment Auxiliaries, and Finishing Auxiliaries. Chemical Fiber Oil is used for lubrication during fiber extrusion. Printing Auxiliaries assist in improving the color fastness and print quality. Pretreatment Auxiliaries are used in fabric preparation processes like desizing, scouring, and bleaching. Finishing Auxiliaries help in enhancing the appearance and durability of textiles. Each segment plays a crucial role in the overall textile manufacturing process.</p></p>
