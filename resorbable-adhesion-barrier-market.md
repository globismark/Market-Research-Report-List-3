<p><p>Resorbable adhesion barriers are used in surgeries to prevent tissues from sticking together post-operation. There are three main types of formulations in the market: Film formulation, Gel formulation, and Liquid formulation. Film formulation creates a protective barrier over tissues, gel formulation is applied as a gel that adheres to tissues, and liquid formulation can be easily sprayed or applied to tissues. Each formulation type has its own unique advantages and is used based on the specific needs of the surgery.</p></p>
