<p><strong>The Diode Heat Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Trap Diodes</li><li>Vapor Trap Diodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diode heat pipes are categorized into two main types: liquid trap diodes and vapor trap diodes. Liquid trap diodes function by using a wick structure to trap and transport the liquid phase of the working fluid within the heat pipe. On the other hand, vapor trap diodes utilize a vapor trap mechanism to prevent the escape of vapor from the heat pipe, enhancing its overall efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in providing effective heat dissipation in electronic devices and industrial applications.</p></p>
