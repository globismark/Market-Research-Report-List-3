<p><ul><li>Vane Automotive Power Steering Pumps</li><li>Roller Automotive Power Steering Pumps</li><li>Slipper Automotive Power Steering Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Power Steering Pumps market consists of three main types: Vane, Roller, and Slipper pumps. Vane pumps are the most common type, utilizing vanes to create pressure and flow. Roller pumps use rollers to press against the cam ring, providing steering assistance. Slipper pumps have a unique design where a slipper slides in and out of the rotor, creating fluid flow. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, with Vane pumps being the most widely used in the automotive industry.</p></p>
