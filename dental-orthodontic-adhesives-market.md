<p><p>Dental orthodontic adhesives are classified into four main types based on how they are cured: chemically-cured type, light-cured type, dual-cured type, and others. Chemically-cured adhesives harden through a chemical reaction, light-cured adhesives are cured using light sources such as UV light, and dual-cured adhesives combine both chemical and light curing methods. The "others" category may include adhesives with unique curing processes. Each type offers different advantages and may be preferred based on specific clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
