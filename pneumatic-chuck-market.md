<p><ul><li>Built-in</li><li>Front-end</li><li>Tie Rod Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic chuck market offers three main types: built-in, front-end, and tie rod type chucks. Built-in chucks are integrated into the machine and provide high-speed rotation and precision. Front-end chucks are attached to the front of the machine spindle and are versatile for various applications. Tie rod type chucks use tie rods for stability and are commonly used for heavy-duty machining. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different industrial needs in terms of performance, ease of use, and durability.</p></p>
