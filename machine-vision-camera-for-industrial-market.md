<p><p>Machine vision cameras for the industrial market are categorized into two main types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture an entire image at once, making them suitable for applications where a complete view is necessary, such as quality control and inspection. Line scan cameras, on the other hand, capture images line by line, making them ideal for inspecting continuous materials like textiles and metals. Both types of cameras play a critical role in enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of industrial processes.</p></p>
