<p><p>The nonwoven cotton market consists of various types, such as Cotton Spunlace and Needle-punched Cotton. Cotton Spunlace is a process where cotton fibers are entangled to form a fabric, resulting in a soft, durable material ideal for applications like wipes and medical products. On the other hand, Needle-punched Cotton is produced by mechanically interlocking the fibers using needles, creating a stronger and more dense fabric suitable for filtration and insulation products. Both markets cater to different needs and industries within the nonwoven cotton sector.</p></p>
