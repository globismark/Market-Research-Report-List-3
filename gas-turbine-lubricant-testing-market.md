<p><p>Gas turbine lubricant testing market types include wear metals, contaminants, oil cleanliness, oxidation, color, appearance, water content, and additive levels. Wear metals testing involves analyzing the presence of metals like iron, copper, and aluminum that indicate wear on engine components. Contaminants testing identifies any foreign substances in the oil that could harm the engine. Oil cleanliness testing assesses the purity of the lubricant. Oxidation testing measures the oil's resistance to oxidation. Color and appearance testing evaluate the oil's visual quality. Water content testing detects any water contamination. Additive levels testing determines the concentration of additives in the oil.</p></p>
