<p><p>Electric injection machines market can be categorized into two main types: vertical injection machines and horizontal injection machines. Vertical injection machines are ideal for applications where the mold can be secured vertically, and are commonly used for insert molding and overmolding. On the other hand, horizontal injection machines are more suitable for applications where the mold is secured horizontally, offering higher efficiency and flexibility in operation. Both types of machines play a crucial role in the plastic injection molding industry, catering to different manufacturing needs.</p></p>
