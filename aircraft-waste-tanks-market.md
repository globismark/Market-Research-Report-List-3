Potable Water Systems are responsible for storing and distributing clean water for drinking and sanitation purposes. Recirculating Blue Water Systems collect and treat wastewater for reuse within the aircraft. Vacuum Waste Systems manage and store waste generated by passengers and crew during flights. These systems are essential for maintaining sanitation and hygiene on aircraft while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
