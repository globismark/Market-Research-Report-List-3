<p><p>The augmented bone graft market consists of various types such as allografts, bone graft substitutes, and cell-based matrices. Allografts are bone grafts taken from a donor and used to promote bone growth. Bone graft substitutes are synthetic materials designed to mimic the properties of natural bone. Cell-based matrices involve the use of cells to promote bone regeneration. These different types of bone grafts serve the purpose of filling bone defects and promoting bone healing in patients undergoing orthopedic procedures.</p></p>
