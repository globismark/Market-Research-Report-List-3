<p><p>Electric pet fence systems can be classified into two main types: in-ground fences and wireless fences. In-ground fences consist of wires installed around the perimeter of the designated area, providing a boundary for the pet. Wireless fences, on the other hand, use a transmitter to create a circular boundary that the pet cannot cross. Both types of electric pet fence systems serve the same purpose of containing pets within a specific area while providing them with the freedom to roam around without the need for physical barriers.</p></p>
