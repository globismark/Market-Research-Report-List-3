<p><p>MR Dampers, also known as magnetorheological dampers, are devices used in engineering applications to control and adjust damping force. These dampers use a magnetic field to change the viscosity of the fluid inside the damper, which in turn adjusts the damping force in real-time based on factors such as vehicle speed and road conditions. MR Dampers are commonly used in automotive suspensions, earthquake protection systems, and various industrial machinery to enhance stability and performance.</p><p>The MR Dampers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.53% during the forecast period.
