<p><p>Vehicle Emission Testers Market consists of various types of equipment used for measuring exhaust emissions from vehicles. NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) Testers use infrared light to detect gases such as carbon dioxide and hydrocarbons. Flame Ionization Detectors detect hydrocarbons through a flame reaction. Smoke Meters measure the opacity of exhaust smoke. Cross Duct Testers measure emissions in the exhaust duct. Emission and Combustion Testers with Chillers analyze emissions and combustion efficiency with the help of a chiller system. Other types of testers include portable emission analyzers and opacity meters.</p></p>
