<p><p>Civil Aerospace Simulation and Training Market includes various types of simulators like Full Flight Simulator (FFS), Flight Training Devices (FTD), and Fixed Base Simulator (FBS). FFS provides a realistic flight experience with full motion capabilities. FTDs are non-motion simulators used for procedural training. FBS are fixed simulators mainly used for cockpit procedures and systems training. Each type of simulator offers different levels of training and simulation capabilities, catering to the diverse needs of the civil aerospace industry.</p></p>
