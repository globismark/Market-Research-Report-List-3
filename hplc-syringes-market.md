<p><p>HPLC syringes are essential tools used in chromatography for injecting samples into the system. There are two main types of HPLC syringes: Autosampler syringes and Manual syringes. Autosampler syringes are used in automated chromatography systems for high-throughput sample analysis, providing precise and consistent injections. Manual syringes are operated by hand and are commonly used in smaller-scale chromatography applications where automation is not required. Both types of syringes play a crucial role in HPLC analysis by ensuring accurate and reliable sample injection.</p></p>
