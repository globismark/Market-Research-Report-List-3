Cat M1 and Cat NB1 are designed for low-power, wide-area networks with enhanced coverage and longer battery life. GPRS modules are widely used for basic communication and are cost-effective. Cat NB2 modules offer even lower power consumption and extended coverage compared to Cat M1 and Cat NB1, making them suitable for various IoT applications. Each type serves specific market needs based on power consumption, coverage, and cost requirements.</p></p>
