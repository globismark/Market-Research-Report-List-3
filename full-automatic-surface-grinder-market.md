<p><p>Full automatic surface grinders come in different types based on their structure and design. The Saddle Type surface grinder features a crosswise movement of the grinding wheel along the saddle, providing stability and accuracy. The Column Type surface grinder has a vertical movement of the grinding wheel along the column, allowing for precision grinding of larger workpieces. Other types of surface grinders may include Rotary and Reciprocating grinders, offering unique functionalities and applications in the metalworking industry.</p></p>
