<p><p>Aluminium fluoride (AlF3) is available in three main types in the market - Dry AlF3, Anhydrous AlF3, and Wet AlF3. Dry AlF3 is the most common type and is produced by reacting aluminium metal and HF gas. Anhydrous AlF3 is produced by removing all water molecules from Dry AlF3. Wet AlF3 contains water molecules and is less commonly used in industrial applications. Each type of AlF3 has specific properties and uses depending on the desired application in industries such as aluminum production and ceramics.</p></p>
