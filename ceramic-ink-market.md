<p><p>Ceramic ink market can be categorized into two main types - Functional Ink and Normal Ink. Functional Ink is specially designed for specific applications like high temperature resistance, conductivity, or anti-bacterial properties. On the other hand, Normal Ink is widely used for general purposes like decoration, labeling, or branding on ceramic surfaces. While Functional Ink caters to specialized industries such as electronics, automotive, and healthcare, Normal Ink serves a broader range of industries including home decor, food and beverage, and packaging.</p></p>
