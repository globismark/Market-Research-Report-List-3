<p><p>Pre-vacuum autoclaves come in two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal autoclaves are designed for larger volume sterilization needs, such as in hospitals or research labs. They have a horizontal chamber layout that allows for easy loading and unloading of items. Vertical autoclaves, on the other hand, are better suited for smaller volume sterilization requirements in clinics or dental offices. They have a vertical chamber orientation that maximizes space efficiency. Both types offer efficient pre-vacuum sterilization capabilities for a variety of industries.</p></p>
