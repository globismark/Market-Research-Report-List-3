<p><p>Fire beam detectors are advanced fire detection systems that use beams of light to detect smoke or fire in large open areas. There are two main types of fire beam detectors: Infrared Beam Detectors and Optical Beam Detectors. </p><p>Infrared Beam Detectors use infrared beams to detect smoke particles in the air, while Optical Beam Detectors use beams of light and a receiver to detect changes in light intensity caused by smoke or fire. Both types are commonly used in warehouses, atriums, and other large spaces where traditional smoke detectors may not be as effective.</p></p>
