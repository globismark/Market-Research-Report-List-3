<p><p>Ferrovanadium market types are distinguished by their vanadium content, with FeV40 containing around 40% vanadium, FeV50 containing 50%, FeV60 containing 60%, and FeV80 containing 80%. These different grades are used for various applications in the steel industry, with higher vanadium content leading to increased strength, toughness, and wear resistance in the final product. Nitrided Ferrovanadium refers to a type of ferrovanadium that has been treated with nitrogen to enhance its properties even further, making it suitable for specific high-performance steel applications.</p></p>
