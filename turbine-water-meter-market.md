<p><p>Turbine water meters are classified into two main types: single nozzle and multi-nozzle. Single nozzle meters have a simpler design with only one inlet and outlet for measuring water flow. On the other hand, multi-nozzle meters have multiple nozzles that allow for more accurate and efficient water flow measurement. Single nozzle meters are typically used in residential settings where lower flow rates are common, while multi-nozzle meters are more suitable for commercial and industrial applications that require higher flow rates.</p></p>
