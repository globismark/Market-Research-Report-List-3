<p><p>Paper coating materials market includes a variety of types such as Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC), Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC), Kaolin Clay, Talc, Starch, Styrene Butadiene Latex (SB latex), Titanium Dioxide, and Wax. GCC and PCC are used for enhancing paper brightness and smoothness, Kaolin Clay for improving opacity, Talc for enhancing printability, Starch as a binder, SB latex for improving strength, Titanium Dioxide for increasing whiteness, and Wax for providing water resistance and glossiness to coated papers.</p></p>
