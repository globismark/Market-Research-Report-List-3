<p><p>Welding transformer market can be segmented into different types based on frequency. High-frequency transformers operate at frequencies above 100 kHz, providing efficient and precise welding capabilities. Intermediate-frequency transformers operate between 1 kHz to 100 kHz, offering a balance of efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Low-frequency transformers operate below 1 kHz, delivering high power output for heavy-duty welding applications. Additionally, there are other types of transformers used in welding applications, each catering to specific welding needs and requirements.</p></p>
