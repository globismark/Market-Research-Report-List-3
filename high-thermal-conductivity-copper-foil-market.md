<p><p>High Thermal Conductivity Copper Foil is available in two main types: Rolled Copper Foil and Electrolytic Copper Foil. Rolled Copper Foil is produced by rolling copper ingots to achieve the desired thickness and surface finish, making it ideal for flexible printed circuits and lithium-ion batteries. Electrolytic Copper Foil is manufactured through an electrolytic process, resulting in a smoother and more uniform surface, making it suitable for various electronic components like transformers and capacitors. Both types offer excellent thermal conductivity and are widely used in the electronics industry.</p></p>
