<p><p>Aircraft de-icing vehicles market consists of two main types of vehicles: sprayers and spreaders. Sprayer vehicles are equipped with nozzles that release de-icing fluid onto the aircraft, effectively removing ice and snow from its surface. Spreader vehicles, on the other hand, disperse solid de-icing agents such as granular materials or pellets onto the aircraft surface. Both types of vehicles play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of aircraft operations during winter weather conditions.</p></p>
