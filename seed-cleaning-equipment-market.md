<p><p>Seed cleaning equipment market can be categorized into two main types- Pre-Cleaning Type and Fine Cleaning Type. Pre-Cleaning Type equipment is used to remove large impurities like stones, dirt, and leaves from the seeds before further processing. Fine Cleaning Type equipment, on the other hand, is designed to remove smaller impurities like broken seeds, dust, and chaff to ensure a higher quality end product. Both types of equipment are essential for achieving high-quality seeds for planting or consumption.</p></p>
