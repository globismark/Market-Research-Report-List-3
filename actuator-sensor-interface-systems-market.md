<p><p>AS-i (Actuator Sensor Interface) Systems consist of several components that work together to create a reliable industrial automation network. The AS-i Gateway/Master is the central device that manages communication between sensors and actuators. AS-i Cable connects all devices in the network, while AS-i Power Supply provides power to these devices. AS-i Slave devices are the actuators and sensors that communicate with the master. Repeaters are used to extend the range of the AS-i network, and Extension Plugs allow for additional devices to be connected.</p></p>
