<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical 3D Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM)</li><li>Melt Extrusion Deposition (MED)</li><li>Powder Binding (PB)</li><li>Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)</li><li>Stereolithography (SLA)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical 3D printing involves various technologies like Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), where material is deposited layer by layer to create a 3D object. Melt Extrusion Deposition (MED) also follows a similar process but with melted material. Powder Binding (PB) compacts powdered material to form a solid object. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) uses a laser to fuse powdered material. Stereolithography (SLA) involves curing liquid resin with UV light. Other technologies in the market include inkjet printing and direct metal laser sintering.</p></p>
