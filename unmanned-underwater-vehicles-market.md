<p><p>The Unmanned Underwater Vehicles (UUV) market consists of Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUV) and Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROV). AUVs are self-propelled underwater vehicles that operate without human intervention, primarily used for oceanographic research, underwater mapping, and defense applications. On the other hand, ROVs are controlled remotely by operators on the surface and are commonly used for tasks such as underwater inspections, maintenance of offshore structures, and deep-sea exploration. Both AUVs and ROVs play a crucial role in underwater missions and exploration.</p></p>
