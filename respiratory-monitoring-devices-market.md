<p><p>Respiratory monitoring devices are essential in managing respiratory conditions. Spirometry devices measure airflow and lung function, while plethysmography devices assess lung volume by measuring changes in pressure. Impulse oscillometry (IOS) and forced oscillation technique (FOT) are used to measure airway resistance and lung function. These devices provide valuable data for diagnosing and managing respiratory conditions such as asthma, COPD, and pulmonary fibrosis. They play a crucial role in monitoring patients' respiratory health and guiding treatment strategies.</p></p>
