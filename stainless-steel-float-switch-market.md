<p><p>Stainless Steel Float Switches are primarily categorized into two types based on their orientation: Vertical and Horizontal. Vertical float switches are commonly used in applications where the liquid level changes vertically, such as sump pumps and water tanks. Horizontal float switches, on the other hand, are used in applications where the liquid level changes horizontally, such as in tight spaces or shallow tanks. Both types offer reliable and accurate liquid level sensing capabilities, but are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
