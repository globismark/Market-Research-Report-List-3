<p><p>Uncooled Thermal Module market is divided into two main types: Amorphous Silicon and Vanadium Oxide. Amorphous Silicon modules are known for their high-resolution images and low power consumption, making them ideal for applications requiring detailed thermal imaging. Vanadium Oxide modules, on the other hand, offer superior sensitivity and temperature accuracy, making them suitable for applications where precise temperature measurement is critical. Both types of modules are widely used in various industries including automotive, aerospace, and defense for thermal imaging and temperature monitoring purposes.</p></p>
