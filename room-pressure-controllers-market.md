<p><p>Room Pressure Controllers are devices used to control and maintain the pressure difference between two areas within a building. There are two main types of Room Pressure Controllers: Fixed and Portable. Fixed Room Pressure Controllers are permanently installed in a specific location within a building, providing consistent pressure control. Portable Room Pressure Controllers, on the other hand, are easily movable and can be used in various locations as needed. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring proper air circulation and maintaining indoor air quality.</p></p>
