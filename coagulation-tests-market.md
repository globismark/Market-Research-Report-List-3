<p><p>Coagulation tests market is categorized into various types based on the technology used for testing. Mechanical coagulation tests utilize physical forces to measure coagulation parameters. Electrochemical tests rely on electrical signals for detection. Optical tests measure changes in light absorption or scattering. Other technologies include immunological assays and molecular tests. Each type of technology offers unique advantages and is used in clinical settings for diagnosing and monitoring coagulation disorders.
