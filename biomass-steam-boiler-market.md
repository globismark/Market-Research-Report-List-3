In CHP systems, the biomass steam boiler simultaneously generates heat and electricity, maximizing energy efficiency. Cogeneration systems, on the other hand, use the waste heat produced during electricity generation for other heating and cooling applications. Both systems help increase energy efficiency, reduce overall energy costs, and lower greenhouse gas emissions, making them attractive options for environmentally-conscious industries.</p></p>
