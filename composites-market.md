<p><p>Composite materials are used in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, construction, and sporting goods. There are several types of composite markets, including layup, where layers of fiber and resin are manually placed and molded; filament winding, where continuous fibers are wound around a rotating mandrel; injection molding, where fiber and resin are injected into a mold; pultrusion, where continuous fibers are pulled through a resin bath and then shaped; and other methods such as compression molding and resin transfer molding. Each market type offers unique advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
