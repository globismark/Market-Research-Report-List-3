<p><p>TLC flash is commonly divided into two main market types: 3D NAND flash and 2D NAND flash. 3D NAND flash involves stacking memory cells vertically, allowing for higher storage capacity in a smaller physical space. This technology is more advanced and offers better performance compared to 2D NAND flash, which stores memory cells horizontally. 2D NAND flash is older technology but is still widely used in more cost-effective storage solutions. Both market types cater to different consumer needs and price points.</p></p>
