<p><strong>The Linen Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluidized Bed Dryers</li><li>Conduction Dryers</li><li>Convection Dryers</li><li>Rotary Dryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linen dryers come in various types to cater to different needs of consumers. Fluidized bed dryers use hot air to dry linen efficiently. Conduction dryers transfer heat directly to the linen through contact. Convection dryers circulate hot air around the linen for drying. Rotary dryers use rotating drums to dry linen effectively. Each type of dryer offers unique advantages in terms of speed, energy efficiency, and effectiveness in drying linen, allowing consumers to choose the best option for their specific requirements.</p></p>
