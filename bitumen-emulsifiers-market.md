<p><p>Bitumen emulsifiers are chemicals used in the production of bitumen emulsions, which are used in road construction. There are three main types of bitumen emulsifiers: anionic, cationic, and non-ionic. </p><p>Anionic emulsifiers have a negative charge and are commonly used for slow-setting emulsions. Cationic emulsifiers have a positive charge and are often used for fast-setting emulsions. Non-ionic emulsifiers have no charge and are typically used for specialized applications. Each type of emulsifier has its own unique properties and is used in specific scenarios in the bitumen emulsifiers market.</p></p>
