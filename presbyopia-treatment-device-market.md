<p><p>The Presbyopia Treatment Device market includes two main types of treatments: Corneal Inlays and Scleral Implants. Corneal inlays are small, transparent devices that are surgically implanted into the cornea to improve near vision. Scleral implants are small, biocompatible lenses that are placed in the sclera to restore the eye's ability to focus on near objects. Both these treatments aim to provide a long-term solution for presbyopia, a common age-related condition that causes difficulty in near vision.</p></p>
