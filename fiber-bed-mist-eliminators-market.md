<p><p>Fiber bed mist eliminators come in two main market types: High-Speed and Low-Speed. High-Speed mist eliminators are designed for applications where there is a high gas velocity, typically above 4 m/s, such as in power plants and petrochemical industries. They are efficient in capturing fine mist particles. On the other hand, Low-Speed mist eliminators are suitable for applications with lower gas velocities, such as in pharmaceutical and food industries. They are effective in capturing larger droplets and solid particles.</p></p>
