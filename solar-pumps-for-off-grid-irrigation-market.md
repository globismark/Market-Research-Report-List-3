<p><p>Solar pumps for off-grid irrigation are becoming increasingly popular due to their cost-effectiveness and sustainability. There are two main types of solar pumps on the market: submersible and surface pumps. Submersible pumps are installed underwater, making them ideal for deep wells or boreholes. Surface pumps, on the other hand, are placed above ground and are more suitable for shallow wells or surface water sources. Both types of solar pumps utilize the sun's energy to power water pumps, eliminating the need for grid electricity.</p></p>
