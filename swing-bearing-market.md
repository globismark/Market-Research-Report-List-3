<p><p>Swing bearings are crucial components in heavy machinery that allow rotation between the upper structure and the undercarriage. There are three main types of swing bearings in the market: toothless type, external type, and internal type. Toothless swing bearings have smooth outer rings without teeth, external swing bearings have teeth on the outside, and internal swing bearings have teeth on the inside. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in different applications based on the specific requirements of the machinery.</p></p>
