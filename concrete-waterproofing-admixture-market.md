<p><ul><li>Crystalline Waterproofing Admixture</li><li>Pore-blocking Waterproofing Admixture</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete Waterproofing Admixture Market is primarily categorized into three main types: Crystalline Waterproofing Admixture, Pore-blocking Waterproofing Admixture, and Others. Crystalline Waterproofing Admixture works by forming crystals within the concrete pores to prevent water penetration. Pore-blocking Waterproofing Admixture works by filling the voids and pores in the concrete to create a barrier against water. Other types of admixtures include hydrophobic admixtures, which repel water, and water-reducing admixtures, which improve workability and reduce water content.</p></p>
