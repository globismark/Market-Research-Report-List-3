<p><p>Antiviral drugs market can be categorized into different types based on their mechanism of action. Reverse transcriptase inhibitors target enzymes involved in the replication of viral RNA, Protease inhibitors inhibit enzymes that are crucial for viral protein processing, Fusion inhibitors prevent viral entry into host cells, and Immune system modulators enhance the body's immune response against viruses. Each type of antiviral drug plays a unique role in combating viral infections by targeting specific stages of the viral life cycle or enhancing the immune system's ability to fight off infections.</p></p>
