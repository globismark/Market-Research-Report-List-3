<p><p>People counting systems come in various types such as IR beam, thermal imaging, video-based, and others. IR beam systems use infrared sensors to detect people passing through a beam of light. Thermal imaging systems use heat sensors to track individual body heat signatures. Video-based systems use cameras to visually count the number of people in a space. Other types of people counting systems may include RFID technology or footfall sensors. Each type of system has its own unique advantages and can be tailored to suit specific needs.</p></p>
