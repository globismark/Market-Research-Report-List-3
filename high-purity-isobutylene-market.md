<p><ul><li>MTBE Decomposition Method</li><li>C4 Fraction Separation Method</li><li>Isobutane Dehydrogenation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Purity Isobutylene market can be categorized into three main types: MTBE Decomposition Method, C4 Fraction Separation Method, and Isobutane Dehydrogenation Method. MTBE Decomposition Method involves breaking down methyl tert-butyl ether to obtain isobutylene. C4 Fraction Separation Method utilizes the separation of C4 fractions to isolate isobutylene. Isobutane Dehydrogenation Method involves the dehydrogenation of isobutane to produce isobutylene. Each method offers a unique approach to obtaining high purity isobutylene for various industrial applications.</p></p>
