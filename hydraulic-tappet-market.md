<p><p>Hydraulic tappets are important components in automotive engines, and they come in various types including plunger, check valve, and one-way valve spring market. Plunger hydraulic tappets use a plunger mechanism to maintain proper engine valve clearance. Check valve tappets have valves that control the flow of oil to ensure smooth engine operation. One-way valve spring tappets have a spring-loaded valve that allows oil to flow in only one direction. Each type of hydraulic tappet serves a unique purpose in the engine system, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency.</p></p>
