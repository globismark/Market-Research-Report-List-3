<p><p>Sensor fusion software market can be classified into Motion Sensor Fusion and Image Sensor Fusion markets. Motion Sensor Fusion involves combining data from various motion sensors like gyroscopes, accelerometers, and magnetometers to provide accurate information about an object's orientation and movement. On the other hand, Image Sensor Fusion involves combining data from different image sensors such as cameras and LiDAR to create a comprehensive and detailed visual representation of the object or surroundings. Both markets play a crucial role in enhancing the overall performance and accuracy of sensor-based systems.</p></p>
