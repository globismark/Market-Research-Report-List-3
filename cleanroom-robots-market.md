<p><p>Cleanroom robots are precision robots designed specifically for use in sensitive environments such as cleanrooms. The three main types of cleanroom robots are Clean Single-Axis Robots, Clean Cartesian Robots, and Clean Scara Robots. Clean Single-Axis Robots provide linear motion in one direction, Clean Cartesian Robots offer three-axis motion for increased flexibility, and Clean Scara Robots provide both rotational and linear motion with a vertical arm. These robots are essential for tasks that require high levels of cleanliness and precision in industries like electronics and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
