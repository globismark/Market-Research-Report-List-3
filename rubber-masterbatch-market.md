EPDM-based type masterbatch is known for its excellent weathering and heat resistance properties. SBR-based type masterbatch offers good abrasion resistance and flexibility. NBR-based type masterbatch is valued for its oil and fuel resistance. Natural rubber-based type masterbatch provides high elasticity and tear strength. Each type caters to different applications and industries, offering distinct advantages and performance characteristics.</p></p>
