<p><strong>The Polymer Polyol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Saturated Aliphatic Polyester Polyol</li><li>Unsaturated Aliphatic Polyester Polyol</li><li>Lactone Polyester Polyol</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polymer polyols are classified into three main types based on their chemical structure: saturated aliphatic polyester polyols, unsaturated aliphatic polyester polyols, and lactone polyester polyols. Saturated aliphatic polyester polyols are used for producing flexible foam with high resilience and durability. Unsaturated aliphatic polyester polyols are utilized in the manufacture of rigid foam with excellent strength and insulation properties. Lactone polyester polyols are known for their high reactivity and stability, making them suitable for applications like coatings and adhesives. Each type of polymer polyol offers unique characteristics and benefits in various industries.</p></p>
