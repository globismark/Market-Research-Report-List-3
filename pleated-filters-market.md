<p><p>Pleated filters are categorized based on their Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value (MERV) rating, which indicates their effectiveness in trapping particles. MERV 7 and below are basic filters suitable for residential use. MERV 8 filters offer better filtration for homes and commercial buildings. MERV 9 to 12 filters provide high efficiency for removing smaller particles. MERV 13 to 14 filters are recommended for hospitals and industrial settings where air quality is crucial. MERV 14 and above filters offer the highest level of filtration for specialized applications.</p></p>
