<p><p>Die attach paste is used in semiconductor packaging to bond the chip to the substrate. There are different types of die attach pastes based on the different cleaning requirements after processing. No-clean pastes do not require cleaning after application. Rosin-based pastes are easy to clean with solvent. Water-soluble pastes can be easily cleaned with water. Other types of die attach pastes include thermally conductive pastes, pressure-sensitive adhesives, and epoxy-based pastes, each catering to specific bonding and cleaning needs in semiconductor packaging.</p></p>
