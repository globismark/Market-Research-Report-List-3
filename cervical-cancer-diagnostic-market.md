<p><p>Cervical cancer diagnostic market includes various types of tests and procedures. Pap testing involves examining cells from the cervix for abnormalities. HPV testing detects the presence of HPV virus, a major cause of cervical cancer. Colposcopy is a procedure that involves examining the cervix with a magnifying instrument. Cervical biopsies involve removing tissue cells from the cervix for further analysis. Cystoscopy is a procedure that allows doctors to examine the bladder and urethra for signs of cancer. These various methods help in early detection and diagnosis of cervical cancer.</p></p>
