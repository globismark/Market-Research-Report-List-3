<p><strong>The Retread Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Cure Process</li><li>Mold Cure Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Retread tire market consists of two main types: Pre Cure Process and Mold Cure Process. In the Pre Cure Process, a new tread is bonded to a buffed casing using a precured tread rubber strip. This method tends to produce a smoother and more uniform finish. On the other hand, in the Mold Cure Process, the tread rubber is applied directly onto the buffed casing and then placed into a mold to cure. This method often results in a more durable and longer-lasting tread.</p></p>
