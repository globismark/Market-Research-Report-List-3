<p><strong>The Replacement Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tires</li><li>Bias Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The replacement tires market includes two main types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires have steel belts running across the tread which provide better fuel efficiency and improved grip on the road. These tires are known for their durability and performance. Bias tires, on the other hand, have cord plies that run at a 30-45 degree angle to the tread. While bias tires are generally cheaper, they tend to have a rougher ride and lower overall performance compared to radial tires.</p></p>
