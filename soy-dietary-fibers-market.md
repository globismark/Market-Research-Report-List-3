<p><p>The Soy Dietary Fibers Market can be categorized into two types - Type I and Type II. Type I market refers to soy dietary fibers extracted from the cotyledon of soybean seeds, which are high in protein and fiber content. On the other hand, Type II market pertains to soy dietary fibers derived from the hull or outer layer of the soybean seed, which contains lower protein levels but higher fiber content. Both types offer various health benefits and are used in food and beverage products for their functional properties.</p></p>
