<p><p>Almond extracts and flavors are available in two main forms - liquid and powder. Liquid almond extract is made by soaking almond nuts in alcohol to create a concentrated flavoring, while powder almond flavor is produced by drying and grinding down almonds into a fine powder. Both forms are commonly used in baking and cooking to enhance the taste of various dishes. The liquid form is preferred for its strong flavor, while the powder form is convenient for storage and transportation.</p></p>
