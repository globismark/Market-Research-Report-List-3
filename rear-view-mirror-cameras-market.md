<p><p>Rear View Mirror Cameras are classified into two main types - CCD Camera and CMOS Camera. CCD Camera, which stands for Charge-Coupled Device, is known for its superior image quality and sensitivity to light, making it ideal for low-light conditions. On the other hand, CMOS Camera, which stands for Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor, is more energy-efficient and cost-effective than CCD cameras. Both types of cameras have their own advantages and are widely used in the automotive industry for improving rear visibility and overall safety while driving.</p></p>
