<p><ul><li>Chemical Imidization Method</li><li>Thermal Imidization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide film for the automotive market can be classified into two types based on the manufacturing method: Chemical Imidization Method and Thermal Imidization Method. The Chemical Imidization Method involves the use of chemical reactions to form the polyimide film, resulting in a film with excellent chemical resistance and thermal stability. On the other hand, the Thermal Imidization Method involves the thermal conversion of polyamic acid to polyimide, producing a film with high mechanical strength and dimensional stability. Both types cater to the specific needs of the automotive industry.</p></p>
