<p><ul><li>Line Die</li><li>Transfer Die</li><li>Progressive Die</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal stamping die market consists of various types such as Line Die, Transfer Die, and Progressive Die. Line Die is a single-station die used for simple shapes. Transfer Die involves moving the part from one station to another for multiple operations. Progressive Die is a multi-station die in which the material moves through a series of stations, with each one performing a different operation. Each type caters to different production needs and complexities, allowing manufacturers to efficiently produce metal components.</p></p>
