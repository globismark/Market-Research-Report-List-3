<p><p>Dimensioning, Weighing, and Scanning (DWS) systems are used to accurately measure, weigh, and scan items for logistics and inventory management. There are two main types of DWS systems: Static DWS systems, which require items to be placed on a stationary platform for measurement, and Dynamic DWS systems, which can measure items as they move along a conveyor belt. Static systems are cost-effective for smaller operations, while Dynamic systems are faster and more efficient for larger operations with high throughput requirements.</p></p>
