<p><p>Submergible mixers come in two main types: Monophase and Three-phase. Monophase mixers use a single-phase power supply, making them more suitable for smaller applications or residential use. On the other hand, Three-phase mixers utilize a three-phase power supply, providing higher power output and efficiency, making them ideal for industrial or larger scale applications. The choice between the two types depends on the specific needs and requirements of the mixing process.</p></p>
