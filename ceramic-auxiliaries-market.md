<p><p>Ceramic auxiliaries market types include rheology modifiers, defoamers, dispersants, wetting agents, and others. Rheology modifiers are used to adjust the viscosity of ceramic materials, while defoamers help prevent foam formation during processing. Dispersants help improve the dispersion of particles in ceramic slurries, while wetting agents aid in spreading the materials evenly. Other types of ceramic auxiliaries may include binders, lubricants, and anti-settling agents, all of which play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and quality of ceramic products.</p></p>
