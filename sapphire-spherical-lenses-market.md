<p><p>Sapphire spherical lenses come in various types depending on their curvature and structure. Concave lenses are thinner at the center and diverge light rays, while convex lenses are thicker at the center and converge light rays. Planoconvex lenses have one flat surface and one curved surface, focusing light to a single point. Each type of lens is used for different applications in industries such as electronics, medical devices, and scientific research, providing precision optics for a wide range of purposes.</p></p>
