<p><p>Overhead Traveling Cranes can be categorized into Single-girder and Double-girder types based on their design and structure. Single-girder cranes have one girder supporting the hoist and trolley, making them suitable for light to medium duty applications. On the other hand, Double-girder cranes have two girders for better support and stability, making them ideal for heavy-duty lifting tasks. Both types of cranes are commonly used in industries such as manufacturing, construction, and logistics for efficient material handling operations.</p></p>
