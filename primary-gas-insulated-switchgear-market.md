<p><p>Primary Gas Insulated Switchgear Market includes two main types: Normal Type and Compact Type. Normal Type typically includes individual components such as circuit breakers, disconnectors, grounding switches, and earthing switches, all housed within a single gas insulated tank. On the other hand, Compact Type integrates all these components into a single compact unit, providing a space-saving solution for installations with limited space availability. Both types offer reliable and efficient operation in medium to high voltage applications.</p></p>
