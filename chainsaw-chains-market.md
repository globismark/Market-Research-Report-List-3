<p><strong>The Chainsaw Chains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Chisel</li><li>Semi Chisel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chainsaw chains are categorized into two main types: full chisel and semi chisel. Full chisel chains have square-cornered teeth that cut through wood quickly and efficiently, making them suitable for tough and dense materials. On the other hand, semi chisel chains have rounded teeth that provide smoother cutting but are less aggressive than full chisel chains. They are ideal for cutting softer woods and are less likely to kick back. Both types cater to different cutting needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
