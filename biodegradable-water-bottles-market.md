PLA is made from renewable resources like corn starch and sugarcane, while PHA is produced by bacteria and is fully biodegradable. Biodegradable PET is a type of polyester that can break down in the environment over time. These materials are becoming increasingly popular due to growing concerns about plastic pollution and the environmental impact of traditional plastic bottles.</p></p>
