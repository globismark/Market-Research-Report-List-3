<p><p>Oxytocic pharmaceutical market can be classified into two types: natural oxytocin and synthetic oxytocin derivatives. Natural oxytocin is derived from the pituitary gland of animals, while synthetic oxytocin derivatives are artificially synthesized in a laboratory setting. Both types of oxytocic drugs are used to induce labor, control postpartum bleeding, and stimulate uterine contractions. Natural oxytocin is often preferred for its similarity to the hormone produced by the body, while synthetic oxytocin derivatives are used when a more controlled and specific dosage is needed.</p></p>
