<p><p>Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) elastic gaskets are available in two main types: Saddle and Waveform. Saddle gaskets have a curved shape resembling a saddle, allowing for a tight and secure seal between two surfaces. Waveform gaskets, on the other hand, have a zigzag or wavy pattern, providing flexibility and resilience in sealing applications. Both types of gaskets offer excellent resistance to extreme temperatures, weathering, and chemicals, making them ideal for a variety of industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
