<p><p>Handheld cable tie tools come in two main market types: manually operated and pneumatic. Manual tools require the user to physically tighten the cable tie around the wires or items being secured. Pneumatic tools, on the other hand, use compressed air to automatically tighten and cut the cable tie, making the process faster and more efficient. Both types of tools are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, construction, and automotive for cable management and organization.</p></p>
