<p><p>Galvanometer scanners are used in laser scanning systems for precise positioning and control of laser beams. They are available in three main types: Single Axis, Dual Axis, and Tria-Axis. </p><p>Single Axis scanners have one moving mirror for scanning in one direction. Dual Axis scanners have two moving mirrors for scanning in two perpendicular directions. Tria-Axis scanners have three moving mirrors for scanning in three dimensions.</p><p>Each type offers different levels of precision and control, catering to various applications in industries such as medical, industrial, and automotive.</p></p>
