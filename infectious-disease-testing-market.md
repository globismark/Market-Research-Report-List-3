PCR is a molecular technique to amplify and detect specific DNA sequences. INAAT is a rapid nucleic acid amplification method. DNA Sequencing & NGS provide detailed genetic information. DNA Microarrays allow simultaneous screening of multiple genes. Other testing methods may include traditional culture-based methods or novel technologies.</p></p>
