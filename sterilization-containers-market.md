<p><p>Sterilization containers come in various types, including filter type and valve type. Filter type containers utilize a built-in filtration system to prevent contamination and maintain sterility of the contents during sterilization cycles. On the other hand, valve type containers have a specialized valve mechanism that allows for efficient steam penetration and removal during sterilization. Both types of containers are designed to provide a safe and effective means of sterilizing medical instruments and equipment for use in healthcare settings.</p></p>
