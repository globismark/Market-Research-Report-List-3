<p><p>Advanced Electric Mops come in two main types: Corded Electric Mops and Cordless Electric Mops. Corded Electric Mops are powered by an electrical cord, providing continuous power without the need for recharging. They are typically better suited for larger areas due to their unlimited runtime. Cordless Electric Mops, on the other hand, are battery-powered and offer more flexibility in terms of mobility and maneuverability.
