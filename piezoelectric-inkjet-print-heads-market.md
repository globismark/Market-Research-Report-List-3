<p><p>Piezoelectric inkjet print-heads in the continuous inkjet market are designed for high-speed and high-volume printing applications, offering superior quality and consistency. These print-heads create a continuous stream of ink droplets that are controlled and directed onto the printing surface, allowing for precise and efficient printing.</p><p>On the other hand, in the random inkjet market, piezoelectric print-heads are designed for more flexible and versatile printing applications. These print-heads can create patterns and images by placing individual droplets of ink in a random and non-linear fashion, allowing for unique and customized printing designs. This market caters to industries that require personalized and creative printing solutions.</p></p>
