<p><p>Office dividers come in two main types: desk-mounted and floor-standing. Desk-mounted office dividers are attached to the edges of desks or workstations, providing privacy and noise reduction. They are ideal for individual workspaces or smaller offices. Floor-standing office dividers are freestanding panels that can be placed anywhere in an office to create separate work areas or meeting spaces. They offer more flexibility in terms of layout and can easily be moved or reconfigured as needed. Both types of dividers help to maximize space and improve productivity in the workplace.</p></p>
