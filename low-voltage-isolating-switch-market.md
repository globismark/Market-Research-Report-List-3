<p><strong>The Low Voltage Isolating Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Column Disconnector</li><li>Double-Column Disconnector</li><li>Three-Column Disconnector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low voltage isolating switches are categorized into three main types: single-column disconnector, double-column disconnector, and three-column disconnector. Single-column disconnectors are the most basic type, with one column to isolate the circuit. Double-column disconnectors have an additional column for added safety and redundancy. Three-column disconnectors offer the highest level of safety with three columns for isolation. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market, offering varying levels of isolation and protection for low voltage applications.</p></p>
