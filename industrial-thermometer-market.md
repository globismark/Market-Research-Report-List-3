Two common types are Bimetallic Thermometers, which use the expansion of two different metals to measure temperature, and Glass Rod Thermometers, which rely on the expansion and contraction of a liquid inside a glass tube. These thermometers are popular choices in industries where accurate temperature measurement is crucial, such as manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and food processing.</p></p>
