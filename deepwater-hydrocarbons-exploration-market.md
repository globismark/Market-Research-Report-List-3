<p><p>Deepwater hydrocarbons exploration market consists of various types such as subsea systems, fixed platform rigs, jack-up rigs, spar platforms, and compliant tower rigs. Subsea systems involve drilling and production equipment located on the seabed, while fixed platform rigs are stationary structures built on the seabed. Jack-up rigs are mobile drilling platforms that are raised above the water on legs. Spar platforms are floating structures used for deepwater drilling, and compliant tower rigs are vertical structures that sway with the wind and waves to reduce stress.</p></p>
