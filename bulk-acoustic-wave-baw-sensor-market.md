<p><p>Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) Sensor Market Types include Resonators BAW and Delay Lines BAW. Resonators BAW sensors are used for frequency control and filtering applications, while Delay Lines BAW sensors are used for signal processing and communication applications. Resonators BAW sensors operate based on the oscillations of acoustic waves within the device, while Delay Lines BAW sensors use acoustic waves to delay an electrical signal. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries such as telecommunications, healthcare, and automotive.</p></p>
