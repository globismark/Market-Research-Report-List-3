<p><p>The automatic hermetic sliding door market is divided into two main types: single leaf and double leaves. Single leaf doors consist of a single panel that slides open and closed, providing a narrow opening. Double leaf doors, on the other hand, consist of two panels that slide apart, providing a wider opening for larger spaces or higher traffic areas. Both types offer hermetic sealing when closed, preventing the passage of air or contaminants, making them suitable for environments requiring high levels of hygiene and security.</p></p>
