<p><p>Right-angle gearboxes are a type of low backlash planetary gearboxes that have a 90-degree angle between the input and output shafts, making them ideal for applications where space is limited or where the shafts need to change direction. On the other hand, in-line gearboxes have the input and output shafts aligned in a straight line, making them suitable for applications where a compact design is required. Both types of gearboxes offer high precision and low backlash, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
